Amazon Prime

Perhaps one of the most well-known features, Amazon Prime offers a plethora of benefits that go beyond just free two-day shipping. With a Prime membership, you gain access to Prime Video, Prime Music, Prime Reading, and exclusive deals on Amazon. Whether you're binge-watching your favorite shows, streaming music, or diving into a new book, Prime has something for everyone.

Amazon Subscribe & Save

Running out of everyday essentials can be a hassle, but with Subscribe & Save, it doesn't have to be. This feature allows you to schedule recurring deliveries of your favorite products, from toilet paper to pet food, at a frequency of your choosing. Not only does it save you time and effort, but you also enjoy discounts on each subscription order.

Amazon Fresh

Say goodbye to grocery store lines and hello to convenient doorstep delivery with Amazon Fresh. This grocery service offers a wide selection of fresh produce, pantry staples, and household items, delivered straight to your door. With features like same-day delivery and flexible delivery windows, Amazon Fresh makes stocking up on essentials a breeze.

Amazon Smile

Shopping for a cause has never been easier with Amazon Smile. This feature allows you to support charitable organizations of your choice every time you make a purchase on Amazon. Simply select your preferred charity, and a portion of your eligible purchases will be donated to them at no extra cost to you. It's shopping with a purpose.

Amazon Lockers

Need a secure and convenient way to receive your packages? Enter Amazon Lockers. These self-service kiosks are located in various locations such as convenience stores, grocery stores, and malls, allowing you to pick up your orders at your own convenience. It's the perfect solution for those who may not be home to receive deliveries or who prefer to pick up their orders on the go.

Amazon Prime Wardrobe

Shopping for clothes online can be hit or miss when it comes to fit and style. With Amazon Prime Wardrobe, you can try before you buy. Simply choose up to eight items of clothing, shoes, or accessories, have them delivered to your door, try them on at home, and only pay for what you keep. Plus, returns are free and easy, making it a risk-free way to refresh your wardrobe.
